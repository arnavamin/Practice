The Saber-toothed tiger is is one of the most famous prehistoric mammals and is part fo the felid, or cat, family.

  • Saber-toothed tigers have an average weight of 150 pounds.
  • The saber-toothed tiger can be of many colors such as tan, brown, yellow, black or white.
  • There were thousands of saber-toothed tigers that existed all across the globe.
